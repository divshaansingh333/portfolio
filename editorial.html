Gamescom: Event Branding & Design

This promotional project for Gamescom, the world's largest gaming event, was designed to capture its vibrant energy and communal experience. The campaign uses a digital vibe with blue and purple colors to be consistent with gaming culture and includes the design of icons, patterns, a poster, and a website to generate interest and encourage immediate action from its audience.

F1 Grand Prix: Promotional Campaign

The objective for this project was to create a 12-page document to be sent out to automobile and racing enthusiasts to promote an F1 Grand Prix. The campaign required an article about a British F1 GP winner, five unique icons, a promotional poster, and the design of a new website homepage.

Tanya Talaga: Rewriting Canadian History

An editorial design project featuring the work and story of Tanya Talaga, exploring her process of uncovering family secrets and telling the unvarnished truth about Canada through her bestselling books.
